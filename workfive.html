The Nature Postcard was a individual task given to me by my school during my time in ITE College Central. The goal of the task was to create a postcard design for showcase.

How i began this design was firstly i went outside, with a canon camera that was loaned to me, took individual photos of flowers and plants using my photography skills. The type of photography i was aiming for was macro photography.

After analysing the photos, i filtered out 6 different photos and using my illustrator skills created the postcard design shown on the left.
