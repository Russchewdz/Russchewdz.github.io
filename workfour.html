In this project my supervisor has tasked me to to work on a electronic direct mail (EDM) for Health Promotion Board.

The target for this EDM was to help the young people eat right and be healthy as major examinations are upcoming.

Throughout this project there were many challenges faced, mostly with working with clients, as they requested many changes and they were unsure of what they wanted. In the end, the final EDM draft was topic surrounding a hunger scale (Shown on the left).
