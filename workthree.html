In this project, I was tasked to create a graphic for an under 21 Footballer, Jordan Sea. He plays for a professional team called Hougang United and wanted a hardcopy canvas Graphic for keeping.

Hougang United Football Club (HGFC) represents an animal called the cheetah.

The core colour of the club is orange and they play in Singapore's top flight league called the Singapore Premier league.
