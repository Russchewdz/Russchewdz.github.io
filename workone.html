Description:

Sun Yat Sen Museum Exhibition [Connection Across Oceans : Early Chinese Mutual Aid Organisations] was one of the biggest projects i have ever contributed in.

In this project i was having my internship with Magicfruit Studios where i was an assistant graphic designer to my supervisor who is the main designer for the project.

The task given for the studio was to design the layout and visuals for the museum using artifects for the exhibition which includes physical materials such as the Masthead Designs, the fonts used, the 3D Layout, Graphic visuals and even Marketing materials.

Though this project was one of the most challenging project i have ever done, the results of the exhibition turned out very well.
